Troubleshooting: Why is the download failing?

If you encounter an error while trying to download, it is usually due to one of the following reasons:

  • Private Account: The video belongs to a private TikTok account. Our system can only fetch publicly available videos.
  • Deleted Content: The creator may have removed the video right before you tried to save it.
  • Invalid Link: Make sure you copied the correct video URL (usually looks like https://vm.tiktok.com/... or https://www.tiktok.com/@username/video/...).
