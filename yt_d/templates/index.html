How to Save YouTube Videos (Step-by-Step)

Downloading YouTube videos is incredibly simple. Just follow these steps on your mobile or PC:

  • Step 1: Open the YouTube app on your phone or go to youtube.com on your computer.
  • Step 2: Find the video or Shorts you want to download. Tap the "Share" icon (the arrow pointing right).
  • Step 3: Tap on the "Copy Link" option.
  • Step 4: Open our Free Downloader website, paste the copied link into the input box above, and click the Download button.

Troubleshooting: Why is the download failing?

If you encounter an error while trying to download, it is usually due to one of the following reasons:

  • Private Video: The video is set to private. Our system can only fetch publicly available videos.
  • Deleted or Copyrighted Content: The creator or YouTube may have removed the video right before you tried to save it.
  • Invalid Link: Make sure you copied the correct video URL (usually looks like https://youtu.be/... or https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=...).
